Initiatives

Patliputra Educational and Welfare Educational Foundation is setting up Integrated Campus catering to Engineering and Polytechnic Technical Education in the 20 Acre Campus in Islampur (near Patna, Bihar).

The campus would be unique in sense of concept and implementation. It would be an ideal synergy between Industry and Academic. The Integrated Campus will be seat to exploring technology and promoting research.

Patliputra Educational and Welfare Educational Foundation believes in integrating the value addition to the three stakeholders.

1. Students : Exposure to Industry Practices

2. Faculty : Continuous up-gradation to Ever Evolving Technology

3. Industry : Highest League of Professionals at Entry Stage , Less Cost on Training

 

 

Campus Details:

Islampur

Islampur is an upcoming vibrant town in Nalanda district of Bihar. It is located on state highway SH-4, barely 60 KMs from Patna and is extreme well connected with train routes to Delhi, Patna and Ranchi.

Islampur is situated between great places of ancient civilization particularly Hindu, Buddhist and Jain. Bodh Gaya, Nalanda, PaavaPuri and Raaj Ghrih (or Rajgir). The serene ambience and the backyard to the new avatar of famed global Nalanda University proposes the perfect location to higher education.
