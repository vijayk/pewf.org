The PEWF Society

Patliputra Educational and Welfare Educational Foundation is a non profitable organization registered under Society Registration Act 21, 1860.

Genesis:

Formation of society dates back to the experience shared by industry professional in management responsibilities in recruitment and grooming at entry stage. The effort undertaken to recruitment at entry stage and training effort required has serious exposed limitation to India Higher Education System.
